## What is Tuta?

Tuta (formerly Tutanota) is a fully encrypted email and calendar app that enables you to keep your private data private [citation:1]. Founded in 2011 in Hannover, Germany, Tuta is run by Tutao GmbH and has grown to over 10 million users worldwide [citation:2][citation:4]. The name "Tutanota" is derived from Latin, combining "tuta" (secure) and "nota" (message) [citation:4].

Unlike traditional email providers, Tuta automatically encrypts not only the content and attachments of your emails, but also the subject lines and your entire address book [citation:1][citation:3]. This zero-access architecture ensures that no one—not even Tuta staff—can access your data [citation:1][citation:5]. All client-side code is open source and available on GitHub for public audit [citation:1][citation:4].

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **End-to-End Encryption** | All data—email content, subject lines, attachments, and contacts—is encrypted before leaving your device [citation:1][citation:3]. |
| **Zero-Access Architecture** | Your encryption keys never leave your device. Tuta has no technical ability to read your emails [citation:1][citation:5]. |
| **Free Account** | 1 GB storage with a free email address—choose from @tuta.com, @tutanota.com, @tutanota.de, @tutamail.com, @tuta.io, or @keemail.me [citation:1][citation:6]. |
| **Custom Domains** | Use your own domain with catch-all support and unlimited email addresses on paid plans [citation:3][citation:6]. |
| **Encrypted Calendar** | Integrated secure calendar with event management and calendar invites [citation:4][citation:6]. |
| **Two-Factor Authentication** | 2FA support for maximum account protection [citation:4][citation:5]. |
| **Open Source** | All client apps are open source and available for independent security review on GitHub [citation:1][citation:2][citation:4]. |
| **Post-Quantum Security** | TutaCrypt protocol replaces RSA-2048 and AES-256 with post-quantum cryptography for accounts created after March 2024 [citation:4]. |
| **Secure Password Reset** | Password reset system that gives Tuta absolutely no access to your data [citation:5]. |
| **No Tracking** | Tuta does not track, profile, or sell user data—no advertising, no behavioral analysis [citation:2][citation:6]. |
| **German Data Protection** | 100% developed and hosted in Germany under strict GDPR compliance [citation:5][citation:6]. |

## Installation Guide

### Option 1: Desktop App (Windows)

Tuta offers full desktop clients for Windows, macOS, and Linux [citation:4].

**Step 1:** Visit the official Tuta website at `https://tuta.com`.

**Step 2:** Navigate to the download section and select the Windows version.

**Step 3:** Download and run the installer.

**Step 4:** Launch the Tuta desktop app and create a new account or log in.

**Step 5:** Your encrypted inbox is ready to use [citation:5].

### Option 2: Use in Browser (No Installation)

**Step 1:** Open `https://mail.tutanota.com` in your browser [citation:5].

**Step 2:** Click **Sign Up** to create a new account [citation:5].

**Step 3:** Choose a plan (Free, Revolutionary, or Legend) [citation:3][citation:5].

**Step 4:** Select your email address from available domains [citation:1].

**Step 5:** Create a strong password—**store it safely as it cannot be recovered without backup codes** [citation:5].

**Step 6:** Save the provided backup codes offline [citation:5].

**Step 7:** Complete the CAPTCHA verification to activate your account [citation:5].

### Option 3: Mobile App

Tuta is available on Google Play and the Apple App Store [citation:1][citation:6].
